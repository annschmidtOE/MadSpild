Get Involved

Getting involved in the fight against food waste can seem a bit overwhelming, where to begin? who to support? how to suppert? and so on .. There are many different ways to get involved, so that everybody can get a chance to help. In our navigation bar you will hopefully find everything you need to know about the different ways you can get involved.
An amazing way almost everyone can become a part of the solution is, to read our "Guide to reduce food waste in private households"
Following those steps really does make a difference, for example only about 3% of food scraps in the U.S. are composted, 24 to 35% of school lunches end up in the bin and about one-third of all food produced for human consumption goes to waste. So by following those simple steps, you will already be contributing to saving our food
Besides that you can find relevant petitions to sign, organisations doing amazing work whom can trust to donate to, and volunteering possibilities all over the world
If you have any questions you can always fill out our contact-form.
