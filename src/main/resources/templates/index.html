Food waste department.

Global food waste is a far-reaching problem with tremendous financial,
ethical and environmental costs. The causes range from bumpy roads to overly-selective customers, but regardless of cause, we can all pitch in to combat this global issue.
An estimated 1.3 billion tonnes of food is wasted globally each year,
one third of all food produced for human consumption,
according to the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) of the United Nations.
The amount of food lost or wasted costs 2.6 trillion USD annually and is more than enough to feed all the 815 million hungry people in the world – four times over.
At GtGG we are not okay with that!
It is our mission to keep this site upated on current food waste data, organisations you trust to donate to, volunteer work around the world and important petitions to sign. Besides that we do make guides for everything from private households to big commercial kitchens on how to reduce food faste.
