About Us

GtGG Food Waste Department exist to help reduce food waste by making all the necessary information available in one place.
GtGG Food Waste Department only exist as an informational site.
Our mission is to help everybody, from households to big commercial restaurants to reduce their food waste.
On our site you will find information about the current food waste situations, guides for everybody to reduce their food loos and opportunities to get involved in different ways, and thereby become a part of the solution.
If you have any questions or feel like wee are missing something you can always contact us, you will find everything you need under "Contact"
We hope you find our site useful and become inspired to participate in the fight against food waste.
