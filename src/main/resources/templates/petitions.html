AVAAZ the world in action
Stop food waste, end hunger		 "It’s a recipe to feed the world -- stop wasting a third of all the food we grow, and stop tens of millions of children going to bed hungry each night. ... We already have a network of politicians ready to table legislation, we just need to build a huge push behind them. Let’s race to build a million-strong petition, submit to the EU consultation before it ends, then work with allies around the world till we win the laws we need." Sign 'AVAAZ' petition
Petitions UK - Create a new tax for retailers that waste food "We need retailers, and especially supermarkets, to stop wasting food every day The Government should create a new tax, set based on the amount of food waste a retailer is responsible for, to incentivise retailers to tackle food waste, and punish those that fail to do so. Each day tonnes of food is sent to land fill by supermarkets. This is just not right and has to change. " Sign 'Petitions UK' Petition
No more food waste to landfill Thames Coromandel "We are calling on the Thames Coromandel District Council to stop sending food waste to landfill and implement other measures that transform waste into a resource. More specifically, we propose TCDC support home-scale food waste solutions, provide kerbside food waste collection and composting, support food businesses' waste minimisation, and deliver an ongoing education campaign." Sign 'No more food waste to landfill Thames Coromandel' Petition
Change.ord - Stop rot! Call on supermarkets to tackle food waste in their supply chain "UK supermarkets and businesses throw out over 7 million tonnes of food annually, before it gets to your shopping basket. That’s enough to lift all the hungry people in the UK out of food poverty. Globally, if food waste was a country, it would be the third top carbon emitter after USA and China. I've started this petition as part of Stop the Rot campaign because I want to see an end to this stark injustice. Consumers are currently asked to do the lion's share of tackling food waste, but many businesses waste more in a day than a consumer does in a year." Sign 'Change.org' Petition
World food program USA - Take the pledge "Global hunger isn’t about a lack of food. Right now, enough food is produced globally to feed the world’s entire population — but more than 800 million people still go to bed hungry each night. Along with conflict, gender inequality, climate change and COVID-19, food waste is a major cause of hunger worldwide. Nearly one-third of all food produced each year is lost or wasted. That is $1 trillion dollars’ worth of wasted food every year that could be going towards feeding hungry people." Sign 'World food program USA' Petition
